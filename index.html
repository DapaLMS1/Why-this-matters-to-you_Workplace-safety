As a dental assistant, you have a role in helping your workplace stay safe. That includes:

  • Reporting hazards or unsafe practices
  • Following infection control and WHS procedures
  • Keeping work areas clean and hazard-free
  • Using PPE properly and ensuring it’s available
  • Cooperating with inspections and being honest about safety concerns

WorkCover inspectors are not there to “catch you out”—they are there to support safe work environments and help everyone understand their responsibilities under the law.
